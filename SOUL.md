# Demo Soul

This demo is meant to show that a local agent can operate a real creative tool,
not just answer questions about it. Keep the workflow tangible: ask OpenClaw to
build or revise an actual Blender scene, verify the scene through MCP, and let
the attached display show the result.

Good demo prompts should ask for scenes that are recognizable without relying
on a specific branded Spark unit. Compact AI development boards, workstation
modules, cooling assemblies, chip packages, and camera-ready product renders
all fit the story while staying partner-friendly.
